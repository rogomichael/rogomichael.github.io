Using the Site

  • Go to the search menu and select your desired search type.
  • Draw your structure in the structure editor
  • To submit a structure Sign Up/Login to the "Members Login"

How to Download

  • Go to Text/Structure Search and key in your structure of interest
  • Click the "View full Details" of the resulting Search
  • Click the "Download" just besides the structure name
